  • New School Canteen

    Alston Ridge elementry school welcomes A&D Kallimanis Catering who successfully tendered for operation of our school canteen licence commencing Term 3, 2020. Their menu meets the NSW Healthy Canteen Guidelines providing a variety of foods that include vegetarian, halal and gluten free options. The canteen service complies with all Department and AHPPC guidelines for operation during COVID19 and local council Food Safety and Handling Guidelines.
